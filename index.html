The growing use of the NCAA Transfer Portal

By Cam Payne | April 21, 2024

The NCAA began tracking the number of atheltes that entered the transfer portal and sucessfully transferred to a new school in 2021. In that time over 30,000 student atheletes across all sports have entered the transfer portal and found a new school. Over 20,000 of those student athletes competed in Football (FBS & FCS divisons), Men’s Basektabll, Women’s Basketball, Baseball and Softball. The five sports combined for over 50 percent of the student atheletes that have been in the transfer portal and succeded in finding a new school to compete at.

Number of athletes that entered the transfer portal since 2021

Since 2021 a total of 20,163 student athletes enterted the transfer portal and enrolled at new schools in Fooball, Men’s Basketball, Women’s Basketball, Baseball and Softball. Football has the most sucessful portal entrants with 8,530 student athletes. Men’s baseketball had eight less sucessful portal entrants from 2021 to 2022 before increasing to an all-time high in 2023.

When athletes enter thr tansfer portal by month

Student athletes typically enter the transfer portal at the end of their sport/team’s season or at the end of the academic year. Student athletes have to tell their school’s compilance office they decided to explore a transfer. Compliance then as two buisness days to enter that athletes’ name into the portal.
